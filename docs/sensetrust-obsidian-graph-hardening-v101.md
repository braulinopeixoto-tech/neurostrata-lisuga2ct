# SenseTrust Obsidian Graph Hardening v1.0.1

Status: implemented as repository automation and documentation; canonical vault application must be validated in the user environment.

## Objective

v1.0.1 is a documental and semantic sprint. It does not change the SenseTrust technical core. It hardens the Obsidian graph so the SenseTrust architecture becomes navigable, demonstrable and explicitly connected across NeuroStrata, VitalStrata, DNDA, BLC and Trust Layer.

## Why the Obsidian graph matters

The graph is a strategic memory asset. It makes architecture, product positioning, audit language, pilot readiness and semantic boundaries visible in one navigable knowledge layer.

## Macro connections

- NeuroStrata connects to DNDA and the NeuroStrata Trust Layer.
- DNDA connects to DNDA Trust Object, Evidence Manifest and Clinical Commit Chain.
- BLC connects hash chain, parent commit, emission hash, audit trail and tenant isolation.
- VitalStrata connects SaaS Core, organizations, usage ledger and future BioDigital Twin.
- SenseTrust connects all layers as the metadata-only trust layer.

## Trail 00 to 14

The sprint makes the SenseTrust trail 00 to 14 navigable through root notes, MOC_SenseTrust and specialized MOCs.

## Boundaries

- No real clinical data.
- No real billing.
- No real legal signature.
- No ICP-Brasil or Gov.br integration.
- SenseTrust certifies integrity, provenance, traceability, document state, emission and public verifiability; it does not certify absolute diagnostic truth.
