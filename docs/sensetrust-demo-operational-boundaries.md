# SenseTrust Demo Operational Boundaries

## Prohibited

- real patient data;
- CPF;
- EEG, qEEG or sLORETA;
- real report content;
- anamnesis;
- medication;
- legal signature;
- ICP-Brasil;
- Gov.br;
- real billing;
- production multi-tenant sensitive data.

## Allowed

- simulated organization;
- simulated user;
- simulated plan;
- simulated certificate;
- simulated hashes and metadata;
- simulated audit report;
- public metadata-only portal.
