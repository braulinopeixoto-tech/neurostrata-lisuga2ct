# SenseTrust Pilot Entry Criteria Board v3.7

## Objetivo
Registrar criterios simulados de entrada para proposta piloto nao vinculante, mantendo relacao com [[Strategic Partnership Conversion Decision Room]], [[Strategic Partner Engagement Control Room]], [[Strategic Partner Readiness Room]], [[Strategic Scale Evidence Simulator]], [[SenseTrust Git Freeze Automation Memory]] e [[FETCH_HEAD ACL Recovery]].

## Limites
Nao aprova uso clinico, contrato, cliente, parceria, LOI real, MOU real, validacao regulatoria, validacao cientifica ou certificacao externa. metadata_only, LGPD e neurodireitos.

## Saidas
Go, Pause, Refine, Non-binding Proposal, Human Review, Legal Review, Scientific Review, Regulatory Review e Block.
