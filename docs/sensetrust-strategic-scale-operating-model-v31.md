# SenseTrust Strategic Scale Operating Model v3.1

## Objetivo

Transformar o portal de prontidao v3.0 em um modelo operacional de escala simulada, com papeis, cadencia, criterios, plano, risco, decisao e revisao humana.

## Origem no ciclo v2.5-v3.0

Deriva do Pilot Control Room v2.5, Evidence Vault v2.6, Certificate Preview v2.7, Closeout v2.8 e SenseTrust v3.0 Institutional Readiness Strategic Scale Gate.

## O que cria

Cria Strategic Scale Operating Model, cadence, RACI, entry/exit criteria, execution plan, governance calendar, risk register, decision log, operational control board, human review path, readiness scorecard, misuse blockers e executive report.

## O que nao cria

Nao cria operacao clinica real, contrato real, cliente formal, parceria formalizada, validacao regulatoria real, operacao comercial, billing, CRM, agenda vinculante ou certificacao diagnostica absoluta.

## Limites de uso

Metadata_only e simulated_only. Go / Pause / Refine / Scale e recomendacao interna, nao autorizacao clinica, juridica, comercial ou regulatoria.

## LGPD e neurodireitos

Sem dado clinico, paciente real, CPF, CID, exame, EEG, qEEG, laudo ou dado pessoal sensivel.

## Relacoes

Relaciona-se com [[Strategic Scale Gate]], [[Institutional Readiness Gate]] e [[SenseTrust v3.0 Institutional Readiness Strategic Scale Gate]].
