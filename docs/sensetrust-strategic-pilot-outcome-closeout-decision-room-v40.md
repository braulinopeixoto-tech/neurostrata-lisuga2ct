# SenseTrust v4.0 Strategic Pilot Outcome & Closeout Decision Room

Objetivo: consolidar o fechamento auditavel de um piloto institucional simulado depois da v3.9 Monitoring & Evidence Intake Room, da v3.8 Execution Governance Room, da v3.7 Pilot Proposal Room e da SenseTrust Git Freeze Automation v1.2 Lean Mode.

A sala sintetiza evidencias simuladas, checkpoints, riscos, desvios, aprendizados e decisao humana Go/Pause/Refine/Stop. Ela prepara v4.1 institutional handoff sem executar operacao real.

A SenseTrust v4.0 nao certifica resultado clinico, cientifico, regulatorio ou comercial. Ela consolida um fechamento simulado, auditavel e supervisionado de piloto institucional, organizando evidencias, riscos, desvios, aprendizados e decisao humana Go/Pause/Refine/Stop.

Critérios: simulated_only, metadata_only, human_review_required, handoff_preparation_only, BLC, Trust Layer, NeuroStrata, VitalStrata, LGPD, neurodireitos e DNDA = Diagnóstico Neurofuncional Dimensional Auditável.

Nao executa: no real outcome, no real pilot, no real clinical operation, no clinical data, no patient data, no biological sample, no EEG/qEEG real, no client, no contract, no partnership, no billing, no revenue, no CRM, no email automation, no regulatory validation, no scientific validation, no external certification, no diagnostic truth certification, no treatment claim, no outcome guarantee, no public health claim.
