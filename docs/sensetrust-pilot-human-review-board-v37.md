# SenseTrust Pilot Human Review Board v3.7

## Objetivo
Centralizar revisao humana simulada para escopo, valor, evidencia, risco e linguagem da proposta piloto nao vinculante. Referencia [[Strategic Partnership Conversion Decision Room]], [[Strategic Partner Engagement Control Room]], [[Strategic Partner Readiness Room]], [[Strategic Scale Evidence Simulator]], [[SenseTrust Git Freeze Automation Memory]] e [[FETCH_HEAD ACL Recovery]].

## Limites
Nao substitui decisao juridica, cientifica, regulatoria ou clinica. Nao cria contrato, cliente, parceria, LOI/MOU, proposta vinculante, CRM real ou email real.

## Decisoes
Go, Pause, Refine, Non-binding Proposal, Human Review, Legal Review, Scientific Review, Regulatory Review e Block.
