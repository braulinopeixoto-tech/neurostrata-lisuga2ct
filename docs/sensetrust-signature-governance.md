# SenseTrust Signature Governance

Status: simulated governance policy.

## Simulated professional signature

The professional signature represents a simulated reviewer role. It includes:

- simulated actor id;
- role;
- simulated organization;
- signature hash;
- signing timestamp.

It is not a legal digital certificate.

## Simulated institutional signature

The institutional signature represents a simulated organization countersignature. It includes:

- simulated institution id;
- simulated institution name;
- signature hash;
- signing timestamp.

It is not an ICP-Brasil or Gov.br signature.

## Legal boundary

v0.7 does not integrate legal signature infrastructure. It creates a technical readiness layer for future integration while avoiding claims of legal certification.

## Private data boundary

Signature metadata must not expose patient identity, CPF, clinical report content, anamnesis, EEG, qEEG, scales, medication, biomarkers or private reasons.
